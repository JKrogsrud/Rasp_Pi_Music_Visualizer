Zach and Jared's Smart Speaker Controller

Hello, and thank you for choosing this smart speaker as your audio visualizer of choice! To use this product, just select an audio source and press play.

  1. Press "Choose File" to select a .wav file from your device.
  2. Press "Upload" to add the audio file. A maximum of 5 audio files may be uploaded at a time.
    Press "Delete" to remove a specific audio file.
  3. With your desired audio track selected, press play to start visualizing!
